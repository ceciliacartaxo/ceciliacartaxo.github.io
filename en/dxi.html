About

I worked on the website redesign for DXI Magazine, an international publication focused on creativity and visual culture. Founded in Valencia, Spain, in 2000, DXI is an experimental platform that offers an independent perspective on contemporary culture, with a strong graphic approach rooted in design.

In addition to its print edition, DXI maintains an active digital presence, publishing daily cultural news and articles. The website redesign was developed through a structured process that included an analysis of the existing platform, research of similar publications, the definition of a new information architecture, and the development of the interface's visual design.
