About

Yanomami Diaries (Diários Yanomami) is a bilingual book (in Portuguese and the Yanomami language) that brings together a collection of texts produced by Yanomami researchers, focusing on the negative impacts of illegal mining exploitation within their territory.



What I did

layout, copy editing, and prepress/finalizing files for printing


Project selected for the 14th Brazilian Graphic Design Biennial
